Political Funds/Affiars Transparency

  1. Our team is planning to use a News API and a Financial Data/Political API

  2. The APIs would inform the user of their legislators' financial transactions as they relate to their political assignments. The idea here is to provide a greater level of transparency to voters into the lobbying funds that could sway how a legislator votes.

  3. The data we are looking to collect from authenticated users is:

    • Name

    • Email

    • Location

    • Political Interests

    • Legislators of Interest

  4. Database entries:

    • USER -> firstName, lastName, email, password, location, politicalInterests, legislatorsOfInterest

    • LEGISLATOR -> CID, firstName, lastName, party, office

    • INDUSTRY -> indCode

    • COMMITTEE -> cmteCode

  1. This site is focused on individuals that are (seeking to be) politically engaged and aware of current political affairs. In addition, it is meant to serve as a tool for individuals to become educated on political issues, local government protocols, and how these decisions are relevant/important.

  2. The two primary goals of this site:
    1. Raise awareness about political issues and events
    2. Keep individuals informed about local government policies and financial affairs of legislators

  3. Our team is motivated to provide a reliable source that offers transparency on the financial affairs of legislators that may swing a legislator's vote. In addition, we strive to raise awareness about relevant political issues for people to stay engaged with political affairs.
